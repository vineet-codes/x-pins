## Overview

This article discusses the original, unrevised edition of Dale Carnegie's work, which contains content that was removed in later editions by his family to make the book more politically correct.

## Key Point

The unrevised edition includes 8 complete chapters on "dealing with women" that were entirely deleted from the revised editions published after Carnegie's death.

## Significance

This resource is valuable for understanding the complete original work and the editorial decisions made in subsequent publishing. The original text provides perspectives that shaped Carnegie's approach to interpersonal relationships.
