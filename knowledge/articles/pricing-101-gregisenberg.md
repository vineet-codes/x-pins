## Key Points

This discussion highlights fundamental pricing strategy questions for SaaS products:

- **Value perception**: What price point do users perceive as fair for a habit tracking tool?
- **Subscription model**: Annual pricing is a common approach for habit tracking apps
- **Market research**: Understanding what users are willing to pay is crucial before launching

## Pricing Considerations for Habit Tracking

Common pricing models in this space:
- **Freemium**: Free basic tracking with premium features
- **Subscription tiers**: $3-10/month or $30-100/year ranges are typical
- **One-time purchase**: Less common but some apps use this model
- **Premium unlocks**: Pay-per-feature approach

## Lessons

For SaaS founders, this is a reminder to:
1. Research competitive pricing
2. Understand your unit economics
3. Test pricing with real users
4. Start lower if unsure, raise prices for new customers
5. Segment pricing by feature tier
